F.A.Q.(Frequently Asked Questions):

Can anyone see my story if i check private when creating my story?

No, if you set your story to be private, no one other than yourself would be able to see it.

Can i change my username?

You can't change your username by yourself, in order to do that you can send us a message in the contact page and we will do it for you.

Can i change my story status to public later?

Of course, you can set your story to be public or private any time you want, however your story will not be placed in the top of global page as stories are sorted by the time of creation, so we suggest you to make a new story.

Can other users see my email adress?

No, no one can see your email adress, so you can use a nickname and write stories basically anonymously

Can i change my password?

you can not change your password by yourself, if you forgot your password, you don't need to worry, contact us in contact page and we will help you restore your account.

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, please leave a message for us using the button below, we will try our best to get in touch with you as soon as possible.
